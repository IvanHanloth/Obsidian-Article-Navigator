# Floating Buttons

Floating buttons hover over the document area—not the sidebar—so they never cover your notes on narrow screens.

## Three styles

| Style | Setting value | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Off** | `none` | No floating buttons |
| **Circular** | `circle` | Round buttons centred vertically on each side |
| **Tall strip** | `tall` | Full-height transparent strips along each edge; only a 30 px click zone is interactive |

Change the style in **Settings → Article Navigator → Floating side buttons**.

## Idle fade

When **Fade buttons when idle** is on, the buttons fade to 12 % opacity after the configured delay (default 3 s). Moving the mouse over them restores full opacity immediately.

Idle fade helps keep the reading area clean while still allowing quick navigation.
